# Verified

### Important Information
The main way to use this is to use Ctrl + F (or whatever your Find shortcut is), then searching the ID, Name, Creator or URL to verify the badge of a creation. 

### Status values:
Pending | Verified 1/2 | Rejected 1/2/3 | Revoked 1/2/3 | Exempt/Custom

**Status meanings:** 
- **Pending:** Verification has started
- **Verified:** 1: Verified, but not stated on the creation yet 2: Verified and stated on the creation
- **Rejected:** 1: Rejected and cannot reapply for 1 month 2: Rejected and cannot reapply for 6 months 3: Rejected and cannot reapply for 1 year + extensive reviewal, and may be completely banned from ever making an application.
- **Revoked:** 1: Applied, got approved, but got revoked. Cannot reapply for 6 months. 2: Cannot reapply for 1 year and requires extensive review 3: May be completely banned or cannot reapply for 1 year+.
- **Exempt/Custom** Please check the ID's notes for more information.
